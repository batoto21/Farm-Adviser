Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is this website all about?

    Farm Adviser is a website that offers many advices and tips regarding with a particular farming that includes fruit farming, vegetable farming, poultry farming, pig farming and fish farming. It will help the customers manage and control activities in farms.

  • How did you come up with this idea?

    We come up with this idea because we want to help our customers and we want our website as useful and relevance at the same time. We also choose this because we think it is easy and as a students who really doesn't have huge experiences in the technological field, we want to develop a website without further complexity.

  • Do you think it is relevant or useful?

    Yes, of course. Though this website is less amazing than the other competitors but we think and believe that this is very useful especially to farmers who don't know how to start their farm businesses. It will help them understand the process in Farming.

  • Can you give us a brief explaination about your experiences while doing your website?

    This website might be too simple to others but for us it is definitely so difficult and hard. We had difficulties in finding specific topic to start-up with our websites and to say that we were pressured would be an understatement. We met a lot of errors during the making-process. It is not easy and never was but hardworks gives best results, so, we did our best and let go of all our worries.

  • Is the design originally yours?

    It's a big NO. The design of this website is originally created by Bootstrap and you can read it in some parts of this website but we made few changes and added new design as well as new topic and information.

  • Can you give us brief background information with the whole team?

    We are a students of Southern Leyte State University-Bontoc Campus taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology majored in Programming. If you want to know more about us please do not stop scrolling because we provide some of our information in some part of this website. Thank You!
